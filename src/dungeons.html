Leto's Lab

Recommended level: 6-7

  • 01.The Gutter
    • Travelling through the portal the party finds themselves in The Gutter filled with bodies. They must crawl upwards to a bigger space above. Two Ironcast Sentinels patrol the pitch-black room, climbing on the roof. They will attack the party.

    2x Ironcast Sentinel
  • 02.Botanic Chamber
    • Leaving the Gutter, the party enters a big, raised chamber. Stairs from the previous one lead to a cliff overlooking the botanic garden below. The garden consists of gigantic pink, glowing mushrooms, few blue crystals and many plants. DC 10 Investigation Check reveals that part of the bodies was used to fertilise the garden. DC 15 Nature Check reveals that the garden is simulating The Underdark. DC 20 Nature Check reveals the crystals to be dendrite. Three Ironcast Sentinels patrol the dimly lit room, climbing on the roof and walls. They will attack the party.

    3x Ironcast Sentinels

    The raised part of the chamber is a platform with 3 entrances.
    1.Alchemical Lab
    2.Main Portal Hub
    3.Body Shuttle
  • 03.Alchemical Lab
    • This small lab is in shambles. Most of the equipment is broken and the main device looks to have exploded. Two Ironcast Sentinels lie dead, seemingly caught in the blast. A ton of plants burned and a handful of shattered glass scattered through.

    A singular metal strongbox lies burned and dented in the corner. DC 12 Thieve's Tools Check opens the box. DC 15 Strenght Check allows the lid to be pried open. The Strongbox has 20 AC and 20 hit points.

    The Strongbox holds:
    1x Potion of Cure Disease
    2x Potions of Healing
    1x Potion of Greater Healing
  • 04.Main Portal Hub
    • The stone doors leading to the hub have a hand of an Iron Sentinel stuck in it preventing it from opening. DC 10 Strenght Check allows the hand to be removed.

    The circular chamber is shrouded in darkness. There is a spherical roof depicting a night sky and a multi-part contraption in the middle of this room.

    The floor of this room is partially covered in sand and it gathers to form a Sand Guardian when the party gets close enough to the contraption.

    1x Sand Guardian

    Next to the teleporter, there is a small ledger with a small note depicting input runes for specific parts of the compound.
    1. Bedroom
    2. The Forge
    3. Main Lab

    "Master Leto this will help you remember the codes - Anton"
  • 05.Leto's Bedroom
    • Leto's Bedroom is a rather large, well-lit room, due to a magical chandelier floating below the roof. Two Ironcast Sentinels are hiding. One under the bed and the second one in an empty wardrobe.

    2x Ironcast Sentinels

    The room consists of a king-sized bed, a large chest, a desk, a comfy armchair and a portrait of Leto and a dwarf lady.

    1. Portrait The woman looks extremely sick. DC 20 Investigation Check reveals a combination of input runes for the teleporter hidden within Leto's eyes.

    2. Chest The chest is a Mimic and holds no loot.

    1x Mimic

    3. Bed The bed has fine silk covers worth 70GP if undamaged. They are highly flammable.

    4. Armchair DC 13 Investigation Check reveals a slight bump under the pillow. Removing said pillow reveals a flask of high-quality bourbon.

    5. Desk The following items lie on the desk:
    -Dagger
    -Coin pouch with 56GP 16SP and 45CP
    -Spell Scroll of Pyrotechnics
    -Shifting Orb
